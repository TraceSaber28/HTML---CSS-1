Sword Art Online

About The Anime

The year is 2022, and gamers have lined up on launch day for Sword Art Online, a hotly-anticipated MMORPG that lets players connect to an immersive virtual reality world with special helmets called Nerve Gear. Kirito and is one such gamer who's eager to jump back into action, having spent a great deal of time as a beta tester. But soon, Sword Art Online's 10,000 players discover that not only are they unable to log out, the only way they can return to their physical bodies is by beating the 100-level tower's final boss - death in the game means death in the real world. Now, with no one else to turn to, Kirito and the other participants must survive the game as best they can.

Protagonists

Born October 7, 2008, Kazuto's mother and father tragically died in a car crash leaving him getting adopted by his uncle and aunt. He became a big video game fan. In the year 2022, Kazuto was one of the chosen out of the 1000 people to be a beta-tester for the very first VRMMORPG (Virtual Reality Massive Multiplayer Online Role-playing game) known as Sword Art Online. He chose the name Kirito as his in-game avatar, and he proved to be one of the strongest and best players in Sword Art Online. As Sword Art Online got it's release on the official release date, Kazuto bought the game and played it. Until Kirito (now in the game itself) realized that Sword Art Online was a death trap. It was revealed by Kayaba, the game's developer, that all 10,000 players that logged in were trapped inside the game, and if they die in the game, they die in real life. The only way to get out of the game is to complete all of the floors in the tower.
Asuna Yuuki the female lead protagonist, she purshased Sword Art Online as an escape from her strenuous life and overbearing parents only to become one of the 10,000 players to be trapped in the world of Aincrad where if you die, your real life body will die too. Having never played videogames she chose her username to be her actual name(not common in the gaming world). Heving no video game experience she meets and partners up with Kirito as they defeat the first floor boss toghether with the raid group. shortly after they part ways and becomes a sub-leader for the Knights of the Blood Oath guild, notable for being the strongest guild in Aincrad. She becomes one of the strongest players earning her the nickname Lightning Flash, which, coupled with her physical attractiveness, have made her well-known to the other players trapped in the game with her.
